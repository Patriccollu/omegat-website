Guida de OmegaT:
Preparation de MS Translator sur Mac


- Log into Microsoft Azure Marketplace:
http://datamarket.azure.com/

*In prime loco tu habera necessitate de registrar un conto Azure Marketplace, si tu non ha jam uno de illos.

- Clicca sur le option My Account al summitate del pagina.
- Presso le fundo tu videra entratas e precios pro:

Primary Account Key
Customer ID

Primary Account Key = microsoft.api.client_secret (necessita pro configurar tu accesso a Microsoft Translator)
Customer ID = microsoft.api.client_id (necessita pro configurar tu accesso a Microsoft Translator)

- Vade a tu directori del applicationes (o ubicunque tu ha tu installation de OmegaT immagazinate sur tu Mac), clicca dextera/cmd+click/two-finger colpa sur le OmegaT executabile e selige "Vide le contentos del pacchetto".
- Clicca sur le plica Contentos e face un copia de reserva/duplicato de tu file info.plist.
- Aperi tu file info.plist in tu favorite redactor de texto e individua le section Proprietates.
- Hic tu addera entratas pro tu valores Primary Account Key e Customer ID de Azure Marketplace (recorda que microsoft.api.client_id = your Azure Marketplace Customer ID e microsoft.api.client_secret = your Azure Marketplace Primary Account Key):

 <key>Properties</key>
       <dict>
           <key>apple.laf.useScreenMenuBar</key>
           <string>true</string>
           <key>apple.awt.graphics.UseQuartz</key>
           <string>true</string>
          --> <key>microsoft.api.client_id</key>
          --> <string>A1234-5678-1234-5678-12345678901</string>
          --> <key>microsoft.api.client_secret</key>
          --> <string>AaBb1234CcDd5678aAbBcCdD1234eEfFgGhH1234</string>
       </dict>

***PER FAVOR NOTA: le flechas supra es SOLMENTE pro fines de illustration. NON los includer in tu entratas real.

- Un vice que tu ha facite le superior entratas, controla duo vices que le valores es correcte e deinde salva e claude tu file info.plist.
- Lancea OmegaT e habilita le option MS Translator:
Optiones > Traduction Automatic... e selige le optiones que gusta.
- Initia a usar OmegaT.
